How you can support us

We are open to any kind of support. Right now we especially are looking for iOS Developers (Bluetooth/BLE knowledge would be a gift).

But every other hand is needed and welcomed.

To get a first glimpse of what our current status is right now, check out the org at github.

To read something about the current project status head over to our confluence page
But please be aware that right now we don't have the time to make everything structured and pretty. (Maybe you want to help there?)

To get working with us, head over to our discord channel. Please say hello in the #onboarding channel and give a small overview over your skillset in the #skillset channel. Have a look at the pinned messages and talk to the leads (see the user list on the right for them) about where you can and want to help.

If you are related to the press or want to outreach to the public in any other way, the simplest way to get in touch is through presse@ito-app.org

For an overview about all links and tools head over to start.ito-app.org

We work 100% transparently

Because we mean it when we say we want to help the world stay safe, every part of our code is open-source.
